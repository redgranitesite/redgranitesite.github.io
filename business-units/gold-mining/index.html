Red Granite Gold Ore Milling

Red Granite Gold Ore Milling Company is located less than 20km along the Zvishavane Bulawayo road, the company provides milling services to miners from Mberengwa, Buchwa and surrounding areas.

The company employs more than 100 employees in total and owns nine stamp mills, two 15 ton Tippers, 2 tractors, Ball Mill, Crusher, CIP system and several cyanidation tanks. We have a reliable backup power supply which covers us in the event of load shedding.
